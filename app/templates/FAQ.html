What kind of content does JantaFile produce?
JantaFile produces investigative video content focused on exposing illegal activities, corruption, and systemic irregularities within Indian society. Our content is strictly based on verified documents, data, and credible sources.
Why don't you show faces in your videos?
We prioritize the truth and the information itself over individual personalities. Not showing faces helps maintain focus on the facts, protects our team and sources, and ensures that the message is the central element.
How can I submit information or tips to JantaFile?
You can submit information confidentially through our "Share Data" page. We have a secure form designed to protect your anonymity. Please provide as much detail and supporting documents as possible.
Is my anonymity guaranteed if I share information?
Yes, absolutely. Protecting our sources is paramount. We employ strict protocols to ensure your identity remains confidential. We will never reveal your identity without your explicit consent.
How do you verify the information you receive?
Our team conducts rigorous fact-checking. We cross-reference information with multiple independent sources, analyze official documents, and consult experts to ensure every detail is accurate and verifiable before it's presented.
How can I support JantaFile's mission?
You can support us by subscribing to our YouTube channel, watching and sharing our videos, engaging with our content, and most importantly, by sharing credible information that helps us expose the truth.
