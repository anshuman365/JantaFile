How JantaFile Works

1. Tip-Off & Initial Assessment

2. Extensive Data Collection

Our team dives deep into gathering all available data. This includes public records, government documents, financial statements, legal filings, and digital footprints. We leverage advanced tools and techniques to ensure comprehensive collection.

3. Rigorous Document Analysis

Raw data is meticulously analyzed. Our experts examine every document, cross-referencing information to identify patterns, discrepancies, and connections that might expose illegal activities or corruption. This is where "the file tells the truth."

4. Multi-Source Fact-Checking

Accuracy is paramount. Every claim, every piece of evidence is rigorously fact-checked against multiple independent sources. We leave no stone unturned to ensure the information we present is irrefutable.

5. Content Production & Presentation

Once verified, the findings are transformed into compelling video episodes. We focus on clear, concise, and impactful storytelling, using visuals and data representations to make complex issues understandable to the common viewer.

6. Public Outreach & Impact

Our work doesn't end with publishing. We aim for real-world impact by ensuring our investigations reach a wide audience, sparking public debate, and encouraging authorities to take action. We believe in empowering citizens to demand accountability.
