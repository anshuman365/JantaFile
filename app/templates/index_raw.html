About Us: JantaFile

"JantaFile" is a platform where we expose information related to illegal or black market activities prevalent in Indian society, which are often hidden. We believe that transparency is the foundation of a strong society, and every effort of ours is a step in this direction.

We don't show faces, because our focus is solely on the truth. Our team works based on in-depth research, document analysis, and data obtained from reliable sources. We verify every claim to ensure that our viewers receive only verified information.

Our objective is not just to expose problems, but to educate the public and help them understand how "the system behind the system" operates.

Our Investigative Process

1. In-depth Research

We gather information from various sources, including public documents, confidential reports, and reliable whistle-blowers.

2. Document Analysis

Every data point and document is carefully analyzed to uncover hidden truths.

3. Fact-Checking

Every claim we make is verified through multiple sources to ensure accuracy and reliability.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the main objective of JantaFile?
JantaFile's main objective is to expose illegal activities and corruption prevalent in Indian society based on documents and data, to promote transparency and accountability.
How do you ensure the authenticity of information?
We verify every piece of information from multiple reliable sources and present only facts based on documents, data, and forensic evidence. Our team follows a rigorous research and fact-checking process.
Is the identity of the informant kept confidential?
Yes, absolutely. We keep the identity of individuals sharing information completely confidential. Our priority is your safety and privacy.
How can I support JantaFile?
You can support us by subscribing to our YouTube channel, sharing our videos, and providing us with confidential information. In the future, we may consider donations or other support options.

Contact Us / Share Information

If you have any confidential information, documents, or a case that needs to be exposed, feel free to contact us. Your identity will be kept completely confidential.
