About JantaFile

Our Mission, Vision, and Values

Our Mission

At JantaFile, our mission is to empower the common citizen by bringing to light the hidden truths and systemic irregularities that affect Indian society. We are committed to unbiased, fact-based investigative journalism, relying solely on verifiable data and official documents. Our goal is to foster transparency, accountability, and informed public discourse.

Our Vision

We envision an India where information is freely accessible, and power structures are held accountable. JantaFile aims to be the most trusted source for investigative content, inspiring a generation of citizens to question, verify, and demand justice. We strive to be a catalyst for positive change, contributing to a more just and equitable society.

Our Core Values

  • **Truth & Integrity:** Unwavering commitment to factual accuracy and ethical reporting.
  • **Transparency:** Revealing the 'system behind the system' through verifiable evidence.
  • **Anonymity:** Protecting the identity of our sources at all costs.
  • **Impact:** Driving meaningful change through our investigations.
  • **Public Service:** Serving the public interest above all else.

Join Us in the Pursuit of Truth

Your support and shared information are crucial to our mission. Together, we can shed light on the darkest corners and bring about the change we wish to see.
